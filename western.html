Progressive Colonial touch

Economical Development

Colonization came with a great improvement in the african economy sector. The industrialisation came with great knowledge, equipment and expertise in the industrial sector. Employment was key factor born from this inheritance.

Education

The education sector experienced a great improvement with this colonial touch. The ability to read, write and fathom concepts is a great ability that brings knowledge and light to any being.

Changable colonial touch

Moral value errosion

The values that africans held so dear have been erroded. Our morals no longer have any meaning to the african setting. From the way we dress, our language and genral mode of conduct.

This can be changed by going back to the drawing board on how much of importance our values were. How our dressing and skin coverage meant a lot to mostly the female gender and most importantly just have the sense of self worth.

Human competition

We used to live in a world of man build fellow man. Any progress to each individual was held with much esteem. It was a society of success for everyone was a benefit to all. Everything was drained to the mud was it became man for himself and God for us all. Man cares for himself and uplifting his own self is dear to thy heart. A neighour in suffering is of none relation to him.

It is not late though, having the mentality of building each others castles and celebrating in people's succes will help build a better nation to live in.
